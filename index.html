What's Included

Every full groom includes a relaxing bath, thorough brush-out, customized haircut, ear cleaning (ear plucking available upon request), teeth brushing, nail trimming, and nail filing whenever your pup is comfortable with the process.

I use only high-quality shampoos and conditioners with every grooming service, and you're welcome to choose the shampoo that best suits your dog's needs at no additional cost.

De-shedding treatments are included with all grooms at no extra charge unless the coat is severely compacted and requires extensive additional work.

To complete the spa experience, every groom also includes a premium facial treatment designed to gently cleanse and refresh your dog's face.

Note — For larger double-coated breeds receiving any trimming or haircut, the deshed price is automatically applied. It helps with the entire grooming process.

About Me

Hi, I’m Morgann!

Hello, I’m Morgann! I am a Colorado native, and spent most of my life in the Brighton area. I’ve been a professional dog groomer for over 11 years, and I’m passionate about helping dogs look their best, feel their best, and enjoy a positive grooming experience. My top priority is making sure every pup is comfortable, safe, and treated with patience and care.

I love working with all breeds and personalities, but I have a special soft spot for the big dogs! Whether your pup is big or small, fluffy or short-haired, sweet or a little spicy, they’re welcome in my salon.

My goal is to provide quality grooming in a calm, caring environment where both pets and their owners feel confident and comfortable. I look forward to meeting you and your four-legged family member soon!

Meet Kylee

My first dog and a very special part of my life. I welcomed her into my life when she was just four months old, and she is now 14 years old. Over the years, she has been a constant source of love, companionship, and joy. Kylee's intelligence, gentle nature, and unwavering loyalty have made her truly one of a kind, and she continues to inspire my deep appreciation for the bond between people and their pets. She has been a life saver to say the least.

Meet Darla

The newest addition to my family and a dog who brings endless energy, adventure, and joy into our lives. I brought her home when she was just eight weeks old, and she quickly became everything I had hoped for in a dog. As a groomer, owning a dog like Darla had always been a dream of mine, not only because of her wonderful personality, but also because she gives me the opportunity to explore my creativity and continue growing my grooming skills. Her playful spirit, enthusiasm for life, and willingness to try new things make every day an adventure. Darla is deeply loved and has become a cherished part of our family.
